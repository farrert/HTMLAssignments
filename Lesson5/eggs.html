A Good Scrambled Egg Recipe

Eggs Green Onion Cherry Tomato
3 1 2

Prep

  • Dice green onion
  • Dice cherry tomato
  • Wrap diced tomato in paper towel

Cook

  1. Place pan over medium-low heat
  2. Put enough oil in pan to thinnly coat bottom
  3. Add the white onion to pan
  4. Stir onions until translucent
  5. Crack eggs into pan
  6. Stir eggs until they start clumping together
  7. Add tomatoes and the rest of the onion
  8. Fold mixture on itself until it looks good enough to eat
