A Look At Inferno

Inferno is a beautiful island with two large mountains in the north and multiple rivers flowing thoughout to make a dozen waterfalls falling from the mountain tops. The island is also covered with trees and flowers even some golden roses are beginning to bloom. There is a bit of a monster invasion starting to take over the mountains in the north, but the science lab is concocting a solution. We are not sure how or when this invasion will be quashed, but there is an awful lot of science going on over there.

Areas of Interest Include:

Outdoor Spa

Located next to the ocean, this spa provides the perfect spot to relax after a long day

Science Lab

This is actually a secret lab, so I can not tell you where it is or what happens there.

Concert Stage

A flashy stage where you will likely find the Flamingo Brothers & the Three Dwarfs rehersing

Arcade

This is where foosball tournaments are held and, like the name implies, there are multiple arcade games to play.
