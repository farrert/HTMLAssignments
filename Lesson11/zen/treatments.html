Rediscover the therapeutic power of touch at Zen Spa & Resort. All Ayurvedic touch therapies are personalized to your unique body-mind constitution and are customized with specific aroma-infused herbal products essences to uplift your spirit, nourish your body and rebalance your emotions.

Popular treatments include:

Scent-sational Relaxation Massage

Classic Swedish massage techniques come together to create the ultimate experience of relaxation. Scent can be changed or removed.

Grand Stone Massage

Warm stones are used to glide over tight muscles to release tension providing for a unique deeper massage.

Thai Body Therapy

Let your body de-stress with the sought after Eastern tradition of Thai. The body is moved and stretched, easing tension, releasing stiff muscles and increasing circulation and energy. Comfortable clothing required.

Anti Aging Facial

Using Sonya Dakar’s Enzyme exfoliation, Custom Green Tea Peel, multiple masks rich with marine-source elastin, berry and yogurt extracts finished with deeply hydrating anti-aging serums reveal fresher, newer, younger looking skin.
